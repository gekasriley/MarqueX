Got Answers
Is the free tier actually free?
100%, no credit card, no trial, no catch. Free lets you browse and join open runs in San Diego forever.
When does The Run launch?
We're launching in San Diego in Q2 of 2026. Everyone on the waitlist gets first access plus a free 30-day Pro trial. No one outside the waitlist gets that offer.
What makes a "verified" player?
Verified players are confirmed real through our check-in system. Show up consistently, build your game history, and you earn the badge. It signals to organizers that you're legit — not a ghost.
Can I host my own run?
Yes. Free users can host open runs in their city. Pro users can create private invite-only runs with full roster control, messaging, and skill-level filters so you always know who's showing up.
Which cities are next after San Diego?
LA, New York, Chicago, and Houston are next. Pro members get automatic access the moment their city goes live — no extra charge, no separate sign-up.
How do I cancel Pro?
Cancel directly inside the app, no phone calls, no dark patterns. You keep full Pro access through the end of your billing period, then it's done.
